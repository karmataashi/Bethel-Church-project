welcome to bethel ag youth ministry :

At Bethel AG Church, our Youth Ministry is a dynamic and supportive community where young people grow in faith, build meaningful relationships, and have fun. Our program offers vibrant worship sessions, engaging activities like fun games and sports, and impactful teachings of the Word of God. We emphasize personal and communal prayer, encouraging youth to pray for each other, fostering a caring environment. Group discussions allow youth to explore their faith and learn from one another, while community service projects instill compassion and social responsibility. Our inclusive environment ensures every young person feels accepted and loved. Join us at Bethel AG Church Youth Ministry to experience a journey of faith filled with worship, learning, and joy, guided by a dedicated team of mentors passionate about helping youth navigate life with faith and confidence.

Highlights from Bethel AG Youth Ministry

Step into the excitement of our Youth Ministry, where faith, fellowship, and fun collide. From vibrant worship to engaging discussions, we offer a dynamic space for young people to grow spiritually and build lasting friendships. Join us as we journey together, exploring faith and discovering the joy of community.

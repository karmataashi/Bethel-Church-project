MINISTRIES

KID'S MINISTRY

Welcome to our Kids Ministry! At Bethel AG Church, we are dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of children in a safe, fun, and engaging environment. Our Kids Ministry provides age-appropriate biblical teaching, creative activities, and meaningful worship experiences designed to help your child develop a strong and lasting relationship with God. Our dedicated team of trained volunteers is passionate about creating a welcoming space where every child feels valued and loved. Join us as we embark on an exciting journey of faith, learning, and discovery together!

MOM'S MINISTRY

Welcome to Bethel AG Church’s Mom’s Ministry, a nurturing and empowering community designed specifically for mothers in all stages of motherhood. At Bethel, we understand the unique joys and challenges that come with being a mom, and we are here to support you every step of the way. Our Mom’s Ministry provides a safe and welcoming space where you can connect with other moms, share your experiences, and grow both spiritually and personally. Through fellowship, Bible studies, practical workshops, and special events, we aim to encourage and equip you to thrive in your role as a mother and woman of faith.

About Bethel

"We exist to reach out the message of Christ(salvation, love, grace, joy, eternal life)
with the lost, needy and helpless.We believe that the message of Christ is the only hope
in this World. Our values are based on the Word of God(Bible) and the teachings
of our Lord Jesus Christ. We believe that every individual has an influence and hence
we have the ability to bring out a change for the betterment of our society."

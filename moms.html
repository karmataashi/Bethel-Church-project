welcome to bethel ag women's ministry :

At Bethel AG Church, our Women’s Ministry is a vibrant community dedicated to spiritual growth, fellowship, and service. We engage in fasting prayers, Bible studies, worship sessions, and testimony sharing to delve deep into the Word of God and inspire each other. Our activities include house and hospital visitations, where we extend God’s love and comfort to those in need, fostering strong bonds of compassion among our members. Join us to grow in faith, experience heartfelt worship, and be part of a supportive community committed to living out God’s Word.

Highlights from Bethel AG Women's Ministry

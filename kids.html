welcome to bethel kids ministry:

At Bethel AG Church, our Kids Ministry is a vibrant, nurturing environment where children learn about God’s love and grow in their faith through engaging Bible studies and storytelling. Our program captures children's imaginations with dynamic Bible lessons, crafts, games, singing, and dancing, making learning about God an exciting and joyful experience. We use age-appropriate teaching methods to ensure that children grasp and enjoy the lessons, breaking down complex biblical concepts into simple, understandable ideas. Our ministry also emphasizes the importance of discipline, manners, and living according to God’s way, teaching children to live lives of integrity, respect, and love. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community where every child feels valued and supported, fostering a sense of belonging and connection through group activities, community events, and family-friendly gatherings. Our dedicated team is passionate about guiding children on their spiritual path, providing them with the tools they need to navigate life with faith and confidence. Join us at Bethel AG Church Kids Ministry, and watch your child flourish in their spiritual journey, growing in their relationship with God and understanding His word.

Watch our kids as they learn, play, and grow in their faith through fun activities and meaningful lessons. Our vibrant community fosters a love for God and a sense of belonging.

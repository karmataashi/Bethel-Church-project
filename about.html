our mission

"The mission of the church is to make disciples by spreading the Word of God and reaching the unreached. This divine mandate is its core purpose and existence."

Who we are

WE ARE BETHEL.
We exist to reach out the message of Christ(salvation, love, grace, joy, eternal life) with the lost, needy and helpless. We believe that the message of Christ is the only hope in this World. Our values are based on the Word of God(Bible) and the teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ. We believe that every individual has an influence and hence we have the ability to bring out a change for the betterment of our society.

Our Belief

the bible

Our Belief: We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

The Trinity

Our Belief: We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

Our Belief: We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully God and fully man. He was born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died for our sins, was buried, and rose again on the third day. He ascended into heaven and will return in glory.

Salvation

Our Belief: We believe that salvation is a free gift from God, available to all through faith in Jesus Christ. It is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.

The Holy Spirit

Our Belief: We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells all believers and equips them for service and spiritual growth.

The Church

We believe that the Church is the Body of Christ, consisting of all believers. The Church is called to worship God, build up the saints, and evangelize the world.
